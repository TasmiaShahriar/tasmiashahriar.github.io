Conversational Qestion Generation using Large Language Models

Large Language Models | In-context Learning | Parametric Statistical Analysis

Research Project, IEC Lab, NCSU

Oct 2023 - Feb 2024

  • Proposes a three-stacked LLM model that utilizes in-context learning capabilities to generate context-aware conversational follow-up questions.
  • A/B experiments with 33 middle school students showed a 13% learning improvement compared to students who did not answer any follow-up questions.

Generating Educational Explanations using Assertion-Enhanced Few-Shot Learning

Research Project, IEC Lab, NCSU

July 2023 - Dec 2023

  • Designed an effective prompting technique for Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate accurate explanations in problem-solving domains reducing hallucinations by 15% in an Intelligent Tutoring System when compared witrh traditional chain-of-thought prompting.
  • Evaluated and compared the quality of educational explanations through survey data analysis from in-service middle school teachers.
